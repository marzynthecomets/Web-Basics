About Me

I’m a gendercreative, fun and funky feminist art director and designer working with brands and nonprofits to do no harm, create cool things, and make the world a better place.

From design to brand identity, illustration and photography, to augmented reality and games, I'm passionate about all forms of creative storytelling. I'm also an advocate for social justice issues, especially DEI and LGBTQIA+ rights. I believe that no matter what your brand statement or mission statement is, the creative work we do together can do good, as well as meet your strategic needs.

Give me a shout via LinkedIn or email me! I love discussing everything from diversity and equity, accessibility, controversial rebrands, and whatever I’m currently binge-watching!
